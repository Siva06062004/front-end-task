Tech Mahendra


Tech Mahindra is an Indian multinational technology company specializing in digital transformation, consulting, and business process outsourcing services.


About the company

  • Tech Mahindra is a leading global provider of information technology services and business process outsourcing solutions.
  • It is headquartered in Pune, India, and operates in over 90 countries worldwide
  • The company offers services such as software development, network services, consulting, and enterprise solutions.
  • Tech Mahindra has a strong focus on emerging technologies like artificial intelligence, blockchain, and IoT (Internet of Things).
  • It serves various industries including telecommunications, healthcare, banking, and manufacturing.
  • The company is known for its innovative approach to digital transformation and customer-centric solutions.
  • Tech Mahindra is a part of the Mahindra Group, a conglomerate with interests in automotive, aerospace, agribusiness, and more
  • With a workforce of over 150,000 professionals, Tech Mahindra continues to be a key player in the global IT services industry.
